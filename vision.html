Vision

A healing arts center that feels like a home base.

The long-term vision is a warm, accessible, community-centered space where healing is not treated like a luxury.

What Finding Eden is becoming

Imagine a YMCA-like community hub, but built around healing arts: somatic movement rooms, journaling corners, music spaces, art workshops, quiet rooms, storytelling circles, youth programs, and trauma-informed community support.

Finding Eden is meant to be a bridge between therapy, community, creativity, and everyday regulation.

The future space includes

Somatic movement + breathwork classes
Writing circles and anonymous storytelling
Art and music therapy-inspired workshops
Quiet rooms for journaling and regulation
Youth and adult community programs
Launch events, retreats, and pop-up healing experiences
