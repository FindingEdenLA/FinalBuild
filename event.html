Launch Event

Return to Self.

Finding Eden’s first 2026 pop-up series: free, bi-monthly community healing gatherings in Downtown Los Angeles.

A sampler of what Finding Eden is becoming.

Return to Self is designed as a low-pressure, beautiful, grounded introduction to the Finding Eden vision.

  • Somatic stretch or movement
  • Sound bath or grounding meditation
  • Journaling / writing circle
  • Creative art corner
  • Optional anonymous story submission
  • Simple community connection without forced sharing

The goal is to start free and accessible, build trust, and invite the right facilitators, volunteers, partners, and supporters into the larger vision.

Needed for launch.

Facilitators

Somatic movement, breathwork, sound, meditation, art, writing, grounding, youth or community support.

Event support

Check-in, setup, clean-up, welcome table, donation table, accessibility support, photography consent help.

Partners

Venues, studios, wellness spaces, arts organizations, local businesses, donors, and community builders.
