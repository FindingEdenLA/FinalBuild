Finding Eden is seeking aligned funders, sponsors, venue partners, facilitators, and community supporters.

Why fund this

Finding Eden sits at the intersection of mental health access, prevention, community care, youth support, creative healing, and trauma-informed programming.

Your support helps us build low-cost events, facilitator stipends, supplies, venue access, youth programming, and the foundation for a permanent healing arts center.
