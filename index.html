Where wellness finds you.

Finding Eden is a community healing space for people navigating trauma, burnout, grief, and the complicated work of becoming themselves — through movement, creativity, storytelling, and real human connection.

Start hereLearn more
What this is

A space built for the parts of healing that happen between therapy sessions.

Almost every person carries something privately. Anxiety. Loss. Grief. Burnout. Confusion. Most people don’t have a safe place to take those things — somewhere that isn’t clinical, expensive, or asking them to perform being okay.

Finding Eden exists to create that place. Movement heals. Creativity heals. Being witnessed without judgment heals. Community heals.

2026 launch rhythm

Bi-monthly pop-up healing gatherings in Downtown Los Angeles.

Finding Eden begins with free, community-centered pop-ups: somatic movement, art, writing circles, grounding tools, storytelling, and quiet connection. The long-term vision is a permanent space, but the first step is building trust through real rooms, real people, and consistent gatherings.
